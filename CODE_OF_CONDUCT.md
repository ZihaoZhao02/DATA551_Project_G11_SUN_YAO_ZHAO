## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment throughout this project.

This dashboard is developed as part of DATA 551 and is intended to simulate a real-world business analytics environment. All contributors are expected to maintain professional conduct in all interactions.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

1. Communicate respectfully and professionally
2. Provide constructive and solution-oriented feedback
3. Be open to different ideas and technical approaches
4. Support collaborative learning
5. Respect deadlines and shared responsibilities

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

1. Harassment or discrimination of any kind
2. Offensive comments related to gender, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, or personal identity
3. Personal attacks or hostile language
4. Dismissive or exclusionary behavior
5. Public or private harassment
6. Intentional disruption of teamwork

---

## Reporting Procedure

If unacceptable behavior occurs:

1. Report concerns directly to team members if appropriate.
2. If unresolved or inappropriate to address internally, contact the course instructor.
3. All reports will be handled respectfully and confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

Individuals who violate this Code of Conduct may be:

1. Asked to correct their behavior
2. Excluded from certain project activities
3. Reported to the instructor in serious cases

---

This Code of Conduct applies to:

1. GitHub repository interactions
2. Pull request discussions
3. Meetings
4. Communication platforms
5. Presentation settings
